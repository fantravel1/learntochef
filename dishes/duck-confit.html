Duck Confit

French slow-cooking mastery. Salt curing, low-temperature cooking, fat preservation. Medieval technique for modern palates.

The 3-Day Technique

Day 1: Salt cure. Day 2: Render fat and slow-cook. Day 3: Rest in its own fat. This teaches patience, salt application, and low-temperature cooking that creates impossibly tender meat.
