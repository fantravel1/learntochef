Biryani

Indian layered rice dish with meat, spices, and fried onions. Dum pukht cooking (sealed pot). A lesson in organization and spice layering.

The Layered Masterpiece

Biryani teaches you spice blooming, marinating meat, partially cooking rice, and dum pukht (sealed pot cooking). The layers build flavor without direct contact. This is organization mastery.

Watch: Biryani Layering

The Dum Pukht Method (90 Minutes)

Marinate Meat: Yogurt, spices, ginger-garlic. 2-4 hours minimum.

Bloom Spices: Toast whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves) in ghee. Fry onions separately until crispy.

Layer & Seal: Rice (half-cooked) ‚Üí marinated meat ‚Üí fried onions. Repeat. Seal pot with dough. Cook on low heat 45-60 minutes.

Rest: 5 minutes sealed. The steam continues cooking and flavors meld perfectly.
