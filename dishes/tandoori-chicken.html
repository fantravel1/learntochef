Tandoori Chicken

Indian spiced grilled chicken. Yogurt marinade with spices, tandoor or high-heat grilling, charred exterior with juicy interior.

The Yogurt Marinade

Tandoori mastery is about marinating thick yogurt with spices, then getting maximum char on the exterior while keeping meat juicy. The yogurt proteins protect the meat from drying out.

Watch: Tandoori Spicing

The Overnight Method

Marinade: Yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, paprika, turmeric, chili powder. Overnight in fridge.

Grill: High heat. Char exterior. Don't move it. Let each side get dark before flipping.

Rest: 5 minutes before serving. The juices redistribute and the meat stays tender.
