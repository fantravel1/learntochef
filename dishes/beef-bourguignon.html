Beef Bourguignon

The ultimate French braise. Tough beef transformed into tenderness over 3 hours. Julia Child's masterpiece.

Organization is Everything

Beef Bourguignon is not complex technically. It's complex organizationally. You need pearl onions, mushrooms, lardons, beef, wine, stock ‚Äî all prepared separately, all added at different times. This teaches you mise en place and timing.

The Recipe

Ingredients (Serves 6)

  • 3 lbs beef chuck, cut into 2" cubes
  • 4 oz lardons (or pancetta)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups red wine (Burgundy)
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 2 carrots, cut into 2" pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 12 pearl onions, peeled
  • 8 oz mushrooms, quartered
  • Bouquet garni (thyme, bay, parsley)
  • Salt, pepper, flour

Mise en Place Checklist

  • ‚úì Beef cut and patted dry
  • ‚úì Lardons sliced
  • ‚úì Pearl onions peeled
  • ‚úì Mushrooms quartered
  • ‚úì Carrots cut
  • ‚úì Garlic smashed
  • ‚úì Aromatics prepared
  • ‚úì Wine measured and ready
  • ‚úì Stock warmed

Step-by-Step (3-Hour Masterclass)

1
Render Lardons (5 min)
Heat Dutch oven, cook lardons until crispy. Remove. This fat is your cooking medium.
2
Sear Beef (15 min)
Working in batches, sear beef until golden on all sides. Season with salt and pepper. Don't crowd the pan. Transfer to a plate.
3
Saut√© Aromatics (5 min)
In same pot, saut√© quartered onions, carrots, garlic until lightly caramelized. Add tomato paste, cook 2 minutes.
4
Deglaze with Wine (10 min)
Pour wine, scrape up all browned bits. Reduce by half to concentrate flavor and cook off raw alcohol taste.
5
Braise (2-2.5 hours @ 325¬∞F)
Return beef. Add stock, bouquet garni. Cover. Braise in oven until beef is fork-tender. Check at 1.5 hours. It's done when you can easily shred a piece.
6
Cook Garnish Vegetables (15 min)
While beef braises, saut√© pearl onions and mushrooms separately. Set aside. Add to braise in the last 15 minutes.
7
Finish (10 min)
Remove beef from oven. Strain sauce, skim fat. Add pearl onions, mushrooms, lardons. Simmer 5 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve with egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
