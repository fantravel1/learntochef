Ceviche

Peru's national dish. Raw fish transformed by citrus juice alone. A lesson in chemistry, fish quality, and minimalism.

Acid Cures Protein

Ceviche proves that heat is not the only way to "cook" protein. The citric acid denatures the fish protein, changing its texture from raw and translucent to opaque and firm ‚Äî exactly like heat does. The result is fresher-tasting than cooked fish because there's no heat-driven flavor loss.

üîë The Chemistry

Citric acid (from lime/lemon juice) lowers pH, which breaks the amino acid bonds in fish protein. The protein molecules unwind and bond with each other, creating that cooked texture. But the flavor? That stays fresh and bright.
