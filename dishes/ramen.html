Ramen

Japanese soul food. Rich broth developed over 12+ hours, perfect noodle texture, and balanced toppings. A lesson in patience and technique.

The Art of Broth

Ramen mastery is all about broth. Tonkotsu (pork bone) broth requires 12-18 hours of simmering to extract collagen and create that signature creamy texture. This teaches you about extraction, bone selection, and patience.

Watch: Ramen Broth Technique

12-Hour Mastery

Hour 0-2: Blanch bones. Clean thoroughly. Start fresh water.

Hour 2-12: Maintain gentle simmer. Add aromatics at hour 8. Skim fat.

Hour 12+: Strain. Cool. Fat rises and sets. This is your base for the perfect bowl.
