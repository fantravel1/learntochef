Paella

Spain's national dish. Saffron-infused rice, seafood, and the crispy socarrat bottom. A lesson in heat management and rice doneness.

The Socarrat Secret

Paella mastery is about understanding rice doneness and building the perfect crispy bottom (socarrat). This teaches you heat control, stock management, and when to let the bottom caramelize.

Watch: Paella Technique

30-Minute Mastery

Saut√© Aromatics: Soffrito base (onion, pepper, tomato). Add rice. Toast for 2 minutes.

Build Paella: Add saffron-infused stock slowly. Add seafood at the right moments. Let rice absorb liquid.

The Socarrat: Increase heat at the end. Listen for the crackling sound. Rice should have a golden, crispy bottom.
