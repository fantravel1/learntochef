Pho

Vietnam's national dish. Star anise, cinnamon, and beef aromatics create an intoxicating broth in 6-8 hours. A lesson in simplicity and spice infusion.

The Aromatic Broth

Pho teaches you how just 5-6 spices, charred onion and ginger, and time can create a broth that's both delicate and complex. The magic is in toasting spices, charring aromatics, and maintaining gentle heat.

Watch: Pho Broth

The 8-Hour Process

Toast Spices: Star anise, cinnamon, cloves, coriander seeds, fennel. Char onion and ginger over open flame.

Build Broth: Beef bones, brisket, charred aromatics, toasted spices. Simmer 6-8 hours. Skim impurities constantly.

Serve: Rice noodles, rare beef (cooked by hot broth), fresh herbs, lime. Each bowl is personalized at the table.
