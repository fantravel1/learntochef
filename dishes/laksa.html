Laksa

Malaysian soul food. Spicy coconut-based broth with noodles, protein, and fresh garnishes. A lesson in spice paste building and layered flavors.

The Spice Paste Foundation

Laksa starts with a complex spice paste: chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, galangal. This paste is fried in oil to bloom the spices, then combined with coconut milk. The result is deeply aromatic and spicy.

Watch: Laksa Spice Paste

30-Minute Mastery

Make Paste: Blend chilies, shallots, garlic, ginger, lemongrass, galangal into a wet paste.

Bloom in Oil: Fry paste in oil for 3-4 minutes until fragrant. The heat releases aromatic compounds.

Add Broth: Stock + coconut milk. Simmer 10 minutes. Add protein and noodles. Serve with fresh herbs and fish cake.
