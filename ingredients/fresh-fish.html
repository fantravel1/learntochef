How to Read Freshness

üëÉ Smell Test (Most Important)

Fresh fish: Smells like the ocean. Briny, not fishy.

Bad fish: Ammonia or strong "fishy" smell. DO NOT BUY.

üëÅÔ∏è Eyes & Gills

  • Eyes should be clear and bright, not cloudy or sunken
  • Gills should be bright red or pink, not brown or gray
  • No brown spots or discoloration on gills

ü§ö Texture

  • Flesh springs back when pressed (no fingerprints)
  • Skin should be shiny and moist, not dull or dry
  • No slime coating (except on certain fish)

üõí Ask Your Fishmonger

For raw consumption: Ask specifically for sushi-grade and confirm it's been frozen properly.
