Why Ingredients Matter

Great cooking is 80% ingredient quality and 20% technique. You can follow every step perfectly, but if your tomatoes are mealy, your olive oil is rancid, or your fish is three days old, the dish suffers.

The Four Rules of Ingredient Selection

  1. Know your source. Where does this come from? Is it in season? Was it treated well?
  2. Understand the grades. Not all olive oil is the same. Not all fish is sushi-grade. Learn the differences.
  3. Invest in the foundation. Splurge on quality salt, oil, and aromatics. You use them every day.
  4. Build relationships. Find a good fishmonger, butcher, and farmer. They become your partners in great cooking.
