David Chang

Founder of Momofuku. Korean-American fusion. Built a global empire by respecting tradition and breaking rules simultaneously.

Respectful Rule-Breaking

David Chang opened Momofuku Noodle Bar in NYC and created a new kind of Asian-American restaurant: serious, bold, unafraid to charge prices for ramen because it's worth it. He respects Korean traditions while being completely original. This balance is why he's revolutionary.

Watch: David Chang on Umami

His Philosophy: "Cook What You Want to Eat"

Chang doesn't follow trends. He cooks what he finds genuine and interesting. If that means Korean-American fusion, fine. If it means high-end ramen, perfect. His authenticity is what resonates with diners.
