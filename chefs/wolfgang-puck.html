Wolfgang Puck

The pioneer of California cuisine. French technique meets local ingredients. Built an empire while staying true to cooking.

The California Revolution

Wolfgang Puck opened Spago in 1982 and changed American cooking forever. He showed that fine dining didn't need to be French. It could be French technique applied to California ingredients: fresh fish, local vegetables, simplicity. He made cooking approachable again.

Watch: Puck on Ingredient Quality

His Mantra: "Good Food Is Good Food"

Puck doesn't separate fine dining from casual. He treats every dish with the same respect, whether it's pizza or a Michelin tasting menu. Quality ingredients, proper technique, respect for flavors. That's it.
