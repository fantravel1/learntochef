The Two-Zone Fire

ğŸ”¥ Direct vs Indirect

Direct heat: Food directly over coals/flame. Use for searing and quick cooking.

Indirect heat: Coals on sides, food in middle. Use for slow cooking large pieces. Covered grill acts like an oven.

3. Rest off heat (let juices redistribute).
