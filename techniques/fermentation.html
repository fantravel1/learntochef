Simple Sauerkraut (Your First Ferment)

Ingredients

1 head green cabbage, 2 tbsp salt

Method

1. Chop: Remove outer leaves. Finely slice the cabbage.

2. Salt: Mix with 2% of its weight in salt (about 2 tbsp for 2 lbs cabbage).

3. Pack: Press into a jar. The cabbage will release liquid. Keep vegetables submerged under the liquid (use a weight if needed).

4. Wait: Cover loosely (allow gas to escape). Taste after 3 days. Ready when sour enough for your taste (3-4 weeks typical).

5. Store: Transfer to refrigerator. Lasts 6+ months.
